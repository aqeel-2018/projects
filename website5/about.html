About EcoTrail

EcoTrail is a simple hiking guide and community built around one idea: nature should feel accessible, not complicated.

Our mission

Make it easy for people to get outside on weekends—without turning it into a huge project.

  • Beginner-friendly
    Routes and tips designed for first-timers and casual hikers.
  • Sustainability
    Low-impact habits that keep trails clean and wildlife safe.
  • Community
    A welcoming vibe: say hi, walk together, enjoy the view.

How we plan hikes

Every hike plan follows the same template so you know what to expect:

  • Difficulty + time
    A quick “what it feels like” note, plus estimated duration.
  • Pack list
    A short list: water, layers, snack, and any special items.
  • Trail etiquette
    Small reminders: stay on paths, share the trail, pack out trash.

Our story

EcoTrail started as a shared note between friends: “Where can we hike this weekend without overthinking it?” Now it’s a small hub for quick routes, calm weekends, and gentle accountability.

  1. Step 1 — Keep it simple
    Short routes, clear notes, and fewer “maybe” decisions.
  2. Step 2 — Show up together
    It’s easier to go when you’re not going alone.
  3. Step 3 — Leave it better
    Respect the place so it stays beautiful for everyone.
